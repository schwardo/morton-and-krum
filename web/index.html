How to play

Goal: Score the most points by typing telegrams across 6 stations.
Each round: (1) A telegram card is revealed with two prompt blanks (e.g., color + zoo animal). (2) Both players blind-bid coal (pass-and-play). Higher bid arrives first; both pay their bid. (3) The 1st-place player picks one blank and chooses a word for it. The 2nd-place player fills the remaining blank. (4) Both players, in turn, assign value tokens (+5 to −2) to any unassigned letters in the two filled words and "transmit" to score.
Tiebreak: bid ties are broken randomly. Bids are paid even if you lose.
